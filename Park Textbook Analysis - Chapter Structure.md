## REVISION STRATEGY SUMMARY

### **High-Yield Topics for Quick Revision:**
1. **Epidemiology** (Chapters 4, 8) - Essential concepts for understanding disease patterns
2. **Communicable Diseases** (Chapters 9-19) - Most frequently tested in exams
3. **National Health Programs** (Chapter 26) - Important for public health perspective
4. **Biostatistics** (Chapter 37) - Critical for research and data interpretation
5. **Environmental Health** (Chapter 6) - Relevant for current health challenges

### **Memory Techniques for Chapter Organization:**
- **Acronyms**: Remember the main parts as "HCCNE" (History, Concepts, Communicable, Non-communicable, Ethics/Research)
- **Numerical Patterns**: Most chapters have 3-5 main sections
- **Page Distribution**: Plan 30-45 minutes per major chapter
- **Quick Reference**: Focus on definitions, key numbers, and program details

### **Exam-Focused Approach:**
1. **Theory Questions**: Focus on concepts, definitions, and classifications
2. **Case Studies**: Practice epidemiological investigation scenarios
3. **Program Details**: Memorize targets, coverage, and achievements of national programs
4. **Recent Updates**: Stay current with COVID-19, newer vaccines, and health policies
